Kurumbapatty Zoological Park

The Krumbapatti Zoological Park in Salem is famous as the Vandalur Zoo of Chennai. The Park is constructed of around 115 hectares in the forest area and in the hilly region of Shevaroyen. It is one of the most wonderful and a beautiful site for the animals and the birds that are kept for the view of the spectators.
The zoo is well known for the peacocks of different varieties and colors. You can spot out cranes and birds of quite different forms that are a mere attraction towards the children. Deer’s and white peacocks are famous in the Kurumbapatti Zoological Park in Salem. There is a small children’s park inside the Zoological Park and the visitors can visit the park form morning 9 am to the evening 5 pm in a day.




Anna Park

Anna Park, located in Salem which is mainly for the small kiddies who can go around during the evening hours to play in the gardens and the play tools that are mainly distributed all over the ground for them. The Anna Park is placed close to the garden lake in Salem District and is mainly loaded with the kid’s events. The greenery of the park really soothes the eyes. The ambience of the park is really relaxing for the visitors. The fun rides are good enough to keep the kids engaged. Nevertheless, at Anna Park you can find cafeteria with quality food starting from snacks to cold drinks.




Paravasa Ulagam Water Theme Park

The Paravasa Ulagam Water Theme Park is located in Salem close to the Trichy NH-7 Highways. The Park is famous for the water games and the children’s activities which the kids form the age of 3 can start playing with. The park also comprises the waterfalls, rain dance, the video games and the biking for the younger generation. Mostly the region is located in a hilly area with a cool atmosphere. Lots of visitor’s a come to this water park along with their children to have a good day.
